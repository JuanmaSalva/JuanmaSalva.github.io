Programa de Convivencia Espacial

Programa de Convivencia Espacial is 2D local multiplayer party-game. Players must compete each other in a zero gravity enviroment during short but envenfull rounds. Players can move jumping from objects or using special objects situated arround the map. There is a variaty of maps and objects to play with over the diferent gamemodes.

The game is made using C++ from scratch using a Entity Component Systen with a State Machine for the diferent modes and menues. In adition, there is a particle system and a animation system for visual efects. For the graphics we used SDL and Box2D for the phisics.

We were a team of 10 people in total working for five months. It was our first big project starting from scratch and the main focus was to experience the workflow of making an actual videogame. Therefore, every member of the group touched nearly every aspect os the game expanding from de UI, to core mechanics, to the base of the "engine".

In general, I'm actually really proud of what we maneged to achived. Just as we started the project, we all got into lockdown and taking into acount that it was our first project, I find that ended up with a decent project that I will allways remember.
