Tanks Showdown

Tanks Showdown is a 1v1 online game in which players must defeat the enemy while maintaining his vehicle alive. Each tank has a base gun and a secondary gun that the player choses from the different options available.

We were two people working on the game and it was developed during our second year of the degree. It's programmed with javascript using Phaser 3 and node.js for the server. We also developed a site around the game to achieve a better user experience.
