RoboPacMan

This is my final degree project. It consists of a replica of the classic game MsPacMan with robots playing on a board using the internet of things as primary technology. The simulation of the game happens on a computer where both the ghosts and MsPacMan are controlled by a custom AI. Through an Arduino that acts as a server, the actions produced in the game, reach each individual robot reproducing an actual game.

The project is made from the ground up. After numerous iterations I developed the robot to a state in which it can actually play a full game of MsPacMan.

This has been probably my biggest and most difficult project to date as it touched areas that were not my specialization at first. But at the end, I'm proud of all that I've achieved by my own. I'm also proud of working together with my University (Universidad Complutense de Madrid) and with Bosch.

The entire project is public on my GitHub.
